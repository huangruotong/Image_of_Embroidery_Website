Modes Overview

Line Mode

Best for images with clear lines and simple backgrounds.

This mode keeps the main lines of the image, so the final result looks cleaner and sharper.

Canny Mode

Best for images with clear outlines and richer details.

This mode highlights the edges and outer lines of the image, making the result look more detailed and layered.

Raster Mode

Best for images with light and dark variation where you want to keep the overall look.

This mode gives darker areas a fuller, filled appearance, so the final result looks closer to a complete design.
